What Makes Judges Say "Wow"

1. Click H gate → Bloch sphere visibly rotates to equator in 3D. Real-time α/β update. Most teams just show a static image.

2. Hit "Measure" → sphere collapses to |0⟩ or |1⟩ with animation + correct probability shown beforehand. Panel can explain why.

3. Step-through mode — apply gates one-by-one and explain what each does to the state vector. No other team will do this.

4. 2-qubit Bell state demo — show entanglement visually with two linked spheres. Say "Einstein called this spooky action." Judges remember it.
